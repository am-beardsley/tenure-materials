Course Description

Developing informative, reader-centered technical communication. Writing, testing, and revising common genres and styles. Writing Instruction in the Disciplines (WID) course. BGS online writing course.

Introduction

From project proposals to social media campaigns, technical communicators are responsible for writing documents that distribute information to the general public. Digital platforms play an essential role for technical writers. This semester, we’ll discuss the work of Sara Austin, the 2023 Magliocco, to critically examine monsters in popular culture. You’ll apply our understanding of Austin’s work to a topic of your choice for a multi-stage project that you’ll work on for most of the semester.
